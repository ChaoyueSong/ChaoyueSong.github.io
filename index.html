Chaoyue Song 宋超越

I am a PhD student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Guosheng Lin and Dr. Fayao Liu. Previously, I received my Bachelor’s Degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Bingbing Ni. I was fortunate to have an internship at Bytedance Seed in 2024, working with Dr. Jiashi Feng and Dr. Jianfeng Zhang.

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning, with a current focus on 3D vision (reconstruction, animation, and generation) and generative AI. If you want to know more about me, please feel free to contact me.
